We are going to look at Three players: MJ, Lebron and Kobe


MJ

MJ played for the Chicago Bulls in his illustrious career. Leading them to 6 championships with a 6-0 record in the finals. In his career he also won several other awards including a gold in the Olympics in the first iteration of America's 'Dream Team'.



Lebron

Lebron James is a name that is usually found in this debate and rightly so. In his career he has played for several teams: Cleveland Caveliers, Miami Heat and the LA Lakers (current team). Over the course of his career he has won 3 championships as well as a gold in the olympics like MJ as well. Lebron's record in the finals is 3-6.



Kobe Bryant

The last player to be discussed, but certainly not the least is Kobe Bryant. Kobe was considered to be the closest thing to MJ that anyone had ever seen. From his mannerisms to his sheer ruthlessness in punishing opponents without any remorse. Over the Black Mamba's career he won 5 championships including 3 in succession something MJ managed to do twice and Lebron not at all. In a recent 2019 interview he said if Shaq (Shaquille O'neal) had his work ethic Shaq could've been the GOAT and he himself would have had 12 championships. His record in the finals is 5-2.



There you have it folks. Although there is a majority voting that MJ is the GOAT the information is there for you to decide for yourselves nonetheless.
